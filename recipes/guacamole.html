Authentic Guacamole Recipe

Description

Guacamole, that Mexican dip that have become a must in every reunion.

In this recipe, I'm going to teach you how to do a proper, authentic Guacamole that is going to bea blast for you and everyone that has the honor to taste it.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 tomatoe
  • 1 onion
  • 2 serrano peppers
  • 1 hadful of corriander
  • half lime

Steps

  1. Cut the 3 avocados i half, remember to remove the center seed carefully.
  2. Slice the halfs in 4 parts.
  3. scoop the sliced avocados with a spoon and put them in a container to blend.
  4. Slice the tomato by the half and chop it, put the pieces in the same container as the avocados
  5. Cut a quarter of the onion and shop it, put the pieces in the same container with the other ingredients.
  6. For the serrano peppers, cut the tail and the tip of both pieces, then chop them very well, put them in the container with the rest of the ingredients.
  7. If you have a food processor, use it to blend the ingredients in the container, otherwhise, use a fork to mash the avocado with the rest of the ingredients, mix very well
  8. Chop the handful of corriander and mix it with the container with the guacamole
  9. Keep blending until your dip looks light green
  10. Add 10 drops of the lime on the guacamole and mix a bit more
  11. Enjoy your guacamole with tortilla chips or with other snacks!
