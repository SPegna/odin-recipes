Pozole Recipe

Description

Here goes the dish description

Ingredients

  • 5 liters of water
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 3 spoons of salt
  • 1 kg of precooked hominy corn
  • 500 grams of pork ribs chopped 4
  • 1 kg of pork pulp
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 14 guajillo peppers seeded and soaked in hot water
  • 2 ancho chili peppers seeded and soaked in hot water
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Chili powder
  • Chopped onion
  • Freighted roman lettuce
  • Sliced radishes
  • limes
  • 10 corn toasts
  • Half cream
  • 100 grams of grated cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the 5 liters of water with 1/2 onion, 4 garlic cloves and the 3 salt tablespoons.
  2. When it boils, add the hominy corn and cook for 30 minutes over hih heat or until the corn bursts.
  3. Add the ribs, the pulp and the bay leaves, cook for hour and half or util the meat is soft.
  4. temove the garlic, onion, bay leaves and the pulp.
  5. Shred the meat and reserve.
  6. Blend the guajillo and ancho chili peppers with 2 water cups, 1/4 onions, 2 garlic cloves and the teaspoons of ground cumin and ground black pepper
  7. strain and pour the mix in the soup, heat for 30 minutes
  8. serve the pozole and mix it with oregano, chili powder, roman lettuce, radishes and drops of lime
  9. You can also accompany with the corn toasts with half cream and the grated cotija cheese
  10. Enjoy your pozole!
