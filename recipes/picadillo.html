Picadillo Recipe

Description

A classic from my country, picadillo can look familiar to some of you, the taste will be seen when you do it

Get yourself ready, tody you're going to do magic with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 spoon of oil
  • 1/4 of chopped onion
  • 1 kg of ground beef
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 tomato Concentrate Cubes with Chicken
  • 1 pieces of marinated chipotle chili
  • 1 chopeed potatoe
  • 1/2 cup of chopped and cooked carrot

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan.
  2. fry the choppes onion util it gets lightly transparent.
  3. Add the ground beef, mix and cook until it's color change.
  4. blend the tomatoe with the concentrate cubes and with the chipotle chili/li>
  5. Add this mix with the beef and onion, cook for 5 minutes
  6. Add the potatoe and carrots and keep cooking for 4 more minutes
  7. Serve and enjoy your picadillo!
